About UK Tiny Houses

Hannah Kent

With extensive experience in the rural economy and residential property sector, Hannah launched UK Tiny Houses to address the evolving needs of the housing market that she has witnessed firsthand.

Having worked closely with both rural communities and property development, Hannah recognized the growing demand for sustainable, affordable housing solutions that don't compromise on quality or design.

UK Tiny Houses represents her vision of bridging the gap between traditional housing and modern living needs, offering innovative solutions that are both environmentally conscious and economically viable.
